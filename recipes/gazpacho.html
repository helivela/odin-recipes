Gazpacho

Description

Gazpacho is a traditional cold soup from southern Spain, especially the Andalusia region. Made with ripe tomatoes, fresh vegetables, olive oil, vinegar, and bread, it is known for its refreshing flavor, smooth texture and for being really easy to prepare.

Ingredients

(6 servings)
  • 1 kg of pear tomatoes
  • 1 italian green pepper
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 ml of extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g of stale loaf of bread
  • 250 ml of cold water
  • 5 g of salt
  • 30 ml Sherry vinegar

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegetables listed above and place them in a blender or a food processor with olive oil, water, bread and sherry vinegar.
  2. Once blended, pass the resulting gazpacho through a fine sieve, pressing with a ladle to obtain a cream without skins or seeds.
  3. Cover it and put it in the refrigerator for a couple of hours to chill thoroughly. The colder, the better!
  4. When serving, you can optionally garnish it with a drizzle of olive oil, small diced cucumber, tomato, croutons or even little chopped hard-boiled egg. Enjoy!
